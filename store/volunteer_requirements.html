Volunteer Requirements

APPLICANTS MUST:                                                  

  1. Be aged 18 or over on the proposed date of arrival in any of the African country;
  2. Be available for an overseas phase of  4, 8 or 12  weeks
  3. Be, in the opinion of the Volunteer Africa team, likely to benefit from and contribute to the programme;
  4. Be able to pass the necessary medical  test must be physically and mentally ready for the trip;
  5. Be able to raise the necessary funding required ( 2500 each month plus your flight ticket plus health insurance;
  6. Be able to communicate effectively in English.
