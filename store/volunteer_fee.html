Volunteer Fee

UPCAWA-SWEDEN will cover 2/3 of the volunteer cost in Africa.

For the above reason all volunteers are requested to pay only a minimum sum of 2,500 Swedish Kronor for those who will be in the field only for 4 weeks (1 month).

Those who will stay long than 4 weeks (8 weeks to 12 weeks) will have more discount and will have to pay only 2,000  Swedish kronor for every month. This is to say if you will be in the field for 8 weeks all you have to pay is 4,000 kro, and for 12 weeks you will pay only 6,000 kro.

 The 2,500 or 2,000 Swedish kronor will cover only the following;

Accommodation from the night of your arrival till the end of your volunteering time.

Feeding, all meals eaten at the residential training centre and throughout your time as a volunteer in the volunteer compound (breakfast, lunch, dinner).

Airport pickup and transportation to residential training centre and back to the airport for your return trip.

Shared kit bought for volunteers accommodation e.g Mosquito nets, cooking equipments, cleaning equipments etc.

Traveling to touristic sites on weekends or having a Party together with staffs and volunteers, all expenditure covered by UPCAWA - SWEDEN.

A substantial donation to the host countries (UPCAWA-CAMEROON NGO, UPCAWA-UGANDA NGO, AND UPCAWA-SOUTH AFRICA NGO, to support the work they are doing in their respective countries.
